Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude

There appears to be no relationship between cloudiness and latitude. Although there are large amounts of data plotted with 0% or 100% cloudiness, those plots span all latitudes with no huge clusters near the equator. The remainder of the data plots are spread across the graph at various combinations of cloudiness percentages and latitudes.
